Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Ascension Wharf is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts to Ascension Wharf, we collect:

  • Account identifiers (username, user ID) from connected platforms
  • Access tokens required for posting on your behalf
  • Content you upload for scheduling (videos, captions, metadata)
  • Posting activity logs (timestamps, platform responses, view counts)

2. TikTok Data

When you authorise our application through TikTok Login Kit, we receive access to:

  • Your TikTok username and user ID (user.info.basic scope)
  • Permission to upload videos to your TikTok account (video.upload scope)
  • Permission to publish videos on your behalf (video.publish scope)

We use this data solely to schedule and post content on your behalf. We do not share your TikTok account data with any third parties. Access tokens are stored securely and are never shared.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your social media accounts
  • Schedule and publish content at your direction
  • Log posting activity for your records
  • Improve the reliability of our scheduling service

4. Data Storage and Security

All access tokens and credentials are stored with encryption. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorised access. Access tokens are stored only on your own device or designated server, not on Ascension Wharf central servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all stored access tokens and account identifiers will be permanently removed.

6. Third-Party Platforms

Our service integrates with third-party platforms including TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, and Snapchat. Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies. Ascension Wharf is not responsible for the data practices of these third-party services.

7. No Sale of Data

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your content and account data remain yours at all times.

8. Cookies

Our web application uses minimal session cookies for authentication purposes only. These cookies do not track your activity across other websites.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@ascensionwharf.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests: privacy@ascensionwharf.com
